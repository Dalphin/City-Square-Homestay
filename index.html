About Us

City SQUARE is famous in Chennai. Whatever it is that you’re travelling for - business or leisure, find delight in every moment of your stay at City SQUARE. Our inviting ambience and warm hospitality coupled with a fleet holistic amenities are set to provide an experience that feels personal. City SQUARE is a home away from home, we view our guests as a part of the family. Feel at home and live the best moments of your life at City SQUARE.

Our Rooms

Our rooms are furninshed with Wardrobe, TV, All other normal amenities. All rooms are Air Conditioned.

Our twin rooms are designed to be as flexible as possible – so they come in different formats. Most feature a super-comfy double or kingsize* Hypnos bed plus a single sofa bed. The rest have two double or kingsize* Hypnos bed.

Our spacious family rooms accommodate up to two adults plus two kids aged fifteen or under. Most of our family rooms feature a super-comfy double or kingsize* Hypnos bed, plus a single sofa bed and a pull-out bed.
